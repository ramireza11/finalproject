ILLUSION

Artist Statement

This game is what I call "illusion". I want the person playing this "game" to get a simulation of being in a illusion

I want them to interact with the real world and the computer world at the same time.

Most of us just look at a screen 24/7 and never look around or do anything interactive with any game outside of our electronics.

The creators of Pokemon Go were an inspiration towards this project because of how they impacted the computer world and the real world.
I want the viewer or player to just have fun. That's all, enjoy this.
